About Us

PT. Indopermata Pangan Jaya was established on June 23, 2023, focusing on the distribution of fresh and frozen fish, with a specialization in salmon. Our company aims to meet market demand for high-quality fish products while ensuring that every step in the distribution process is carried out with high standards of hygiene and safety.

Our Mission

  • Providing high-quality fresh and frozen fish products with the highest standards of safety and hygiene.
  • Ensuring product availability through extensive and efficient distribution channels, reaching Modern Markets, Hotels, Restaurants, Cafés, Catering services, and direct consumers.

Our Vision

To become a leading company committed to sustainable growth, providing high-quality fresh and frozen fish supplies to people across the nation. We strive to serve various market segments through collaboration with the Modern Market sector, Hotels, Restaurants, Cafés, and Catering, and to be the top choice in fulfilling the demand for healthy, safe, and affordable seafood.
